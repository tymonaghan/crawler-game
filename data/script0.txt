Following the 1993 bombing of the World Trade Center, the US Congress and the President authorized the creation of a new branch of the Central Intelligence Agency. Headquartered in Washington DC, this new unit operates under a mandate to investigate both foreign and domestic terrorists within US borders, with field offices in twelve major American cities. Its Agents are recruited from the top ranks of US Law Enforcement and Special Forces, with expertise in electronic surveillance, human intelligence, undercover operations, and direct action. You are just such an agent, newly recruited for the Counter Terrorist Unit - Chicago Field Office...
